Puzzle Inu (PINU) is a utility token designed on Binance smart chain (BEP20). Smart Contract deployed on bscscan. The contract is verified and published for all. Anyone can check by findings our official contract address.

Puzzle Inu - PINU contract developed by pinksale nominated SAFU developer. SAFU contract ensures the security of investment and anti-dump method. Contract audited by the partner audit team of pinksale. They issued a certificate after passing successfully all measuring scales. No Mint function, no manipulation system finds. BNB Rewards

Offering BNB rewards to holders means they will get value back directly in their wallet with the automatic rewards system. A constant way of passive income. This allows people to easily fill up with more Puzzle Inu or allow them to continuously receive back a % of their investment for doing nothing else other than holding Puzzle Inu - PINU.

Buy Back

The BuyBack feature will be done manually by the team. The buy-back will be used to predominantly buy Puzzle Inu and then send it to the dead wallet removing it from circulation forever. The value of this to holders of Puzzle Inu means not only will Puzzle Inu help to push the value of Puzzle Inu but also burning the Puzzle Inu purchased means that the value put into Puzzle Inu will remain. The BuyBack wallet will also be used if needed to implement the same for Puzzle Inu if the project needs a boost.

LOCKED LIQUIDITY

"Liquidity" is central to how PancakeSwap's Exchange works. PINU liquidity is locked on Pinksale for 3 years. You can add liquidity for any token pair by staking both through the Liquidity page. In return for adding liquidity, you'll receive trading fees for that pair, and receive LP Tokens you can stake in Farms to earn rewards! On PINU a % of buy and sales tax will be added to liquidity automatically. Which is one of the most important keys to keeping the project stable for a good long term.
